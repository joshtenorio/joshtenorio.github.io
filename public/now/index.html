What I’m doing now

This is my now page.

Formula SAE

I compete on Sun Devil Motorsports, ASU’s Formula SAE team, as the Data Acquisition team lead, where we are responsible for integrating sensors into a Formula-style race car to validate its design and extract the most performance out of it. Currently we are working on a redesign of our data acquisition system to allow for easy modifications, simpler cable runs, and collecting data from the ECU. Our code and PCB designs can be found on GitHub while the team website can be found here.

18x18az

I volunteer with 18x18az, a non-profit specializing in hosting high-quality VEX Robotics Competition (VRC) tournaments. Most recently, we’ve successfully hosted both state championships for the 2022-23 season. We’ve also been developing a companion software to Tournament Manager, the program used to run VRC tournaments, to allow us to make custom audience displays, volunteer interfaces, and automate livestream management.

Arizona State University

I’m currently in my fourth year at ASU studying Computer Science and Mathematics, and I’m expecting to graduate in May 2024.
